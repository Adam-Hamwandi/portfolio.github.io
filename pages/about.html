    UI UX Designer & Software Engineer

    As an experienced web designer and UX/UI specialist, I am passionate about creating user-centered designs that solve real-world problems. With 7 years of experience in the industry, I have honed my skills in everything from wireframing and prototyping to front-end development and user testing. My background in freelancing designer & engineer has equipped me with a deep understanding of the design process and the ability to think creatively and strategically about design solutions. I am well-versed in a range of design tools, including "Figma, Adobe XD, Miro, JustInMind, Adobe InDesign and so much more". I believe that collaboration is key to successful design, and I enjoy working closely with clients and team members to ensure that all stakeholders' needs are met. Whether I'm designing a new website, developing an app, or creating a branding strategy, I strive to deliver the highest quality work that meets both my clients' and users' needs.

    If you're interested in working together or learning more about my design process, feel free to reach out. Let's create something amazing together!
